### Project Submission Process

1. **Fork this repository** (if required by organizers) or create a folder in the `submissions/` directory
2. **Create a folder** with your team name: `submissions/team-name/`
3. **Add your project files** to your team folder

5. **Submit a pull request** before the deadline (if using fork model)

### README.md Requirements

Your project README.md must include:

1. **Project Title**
2. **Team Members** (with names and student IDs)
3. **Problem Statement** (which challenge you're addressing)
4. **Description** (what your project does)
5. **Technologies Used**
6. **Installation Instructions**
7. **Usage Guide**
8. **Screenshots/Demo** (if applicable)
9. **Future Enhancements** (optional)
